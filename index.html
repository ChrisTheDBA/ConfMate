Let your attendees create their own schedule

You are organizing a conference ? Have a mobile web app to help your attendees browse talks, speakers, location, and interact with your conference.


Features
Free & Premium plans

Why do you need a mobile web app for your attendees ?

I attended last RailsConf 2013 in Portland, OR. I had the need to create my own schedule, as there were several talks at the same moment, but the official mobile app wasn't able to do this and was not really usable. So I created one. Your attendees have the same need.

Features

Browse talks and full schedule offline
See the #twitter feed of your conference
Let your users favorite talks and have a custom schedule
See speakers details
Browse conference details like location, hours, organization team...

Free Version for any non-profit conferences

If you are a non-profit organization, you can use it for free. You have to follow the instructions and host it yourself.

Premium Version for only 299$

For any other type of conference, you will need to get the premium version.

The premium version is 299$. You will have a custom theme development, illimited 24h support, and your application will be hosted on a new domain if you want to, or you can host it on your server. Your application can also be published on the App Store and Google Play Android for an extra 49$.
